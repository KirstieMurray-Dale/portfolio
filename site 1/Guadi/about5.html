About me:

 

 

I'm a student artist, animator and figure skater based in Sheffield!


You will be able to tell from my various creative works that I'm interested in all things whimsical. My artistic ventures generally centre around creating/communicating a narrative as inspiring tales are what motivate me in almost every aspect of life.


This website has been created on Dreamweaver for my Creative Media Cambridge Technical unit 4, you should be able to navigate around the site to inspect some of my recent creative work by following the links above- I hope you enjoy viewing my work!
